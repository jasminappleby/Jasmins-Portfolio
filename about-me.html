    • My Life

    Born on June 26th 2000 in Luton, Bedfordshire. I spent 18 years of my life solely in Luton.

    Throughout my childhood, I collected plushies, played video games and ate pizza... not much has changed.

    In 2018, I flew the nest and decided to go to University, De Montfort University in Leicester. This is where I studied Computer Science for three years.

    Moving to University was my first time living in and renting a place to live. Besides the pandemic, I really enjoyed the freedom I got from living away from home.

    Now, I am currently swinging inbetween both Luton and Leicester, because both are home to me!
